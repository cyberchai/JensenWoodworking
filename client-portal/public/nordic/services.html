Wood Selection

Explore Wood Options

We work exclusively with responsibly sourced hardwoods. Each species has its own character, grain pattern, and color — from the subtle grain of Ash, to the timeless appeal of Oak, to the rich character of Walnut.

Black Walnut

Rich, dark brown with beautiful grain patterns. Known for its durability and elegant appearance. Perfect for statement pieces like dining tables and islands.

Characteristics: Dark brown, fine grain, very durable
Best for: Dining tables, countertops, islands

Cherry

Warm reddish-brown that deepens with age. Smooth grain and excellent workability. Ideal for elegant furniture and kitchen applications.

Characteristics: Reddish-brown, smooth grain, ages beautifully
Best for: Countertops, dining tables, mantels

White Oak

Light golden to medium brown with distinctive ray fleck patterns. Extremely durable and water-resistant. Perfect for butcher blocks and heavy-use surfaces.

Characteristics: Light brown, ray fleck pattern, very durable
Best for: Butcher blocks, countertops, islands

Maple

Light, creamy color with subtle grain. Hard and durable, making it ideal for high-traffic areas. Classic choice for kitchen applications.

Characteristics: Light cream, subtle grain, very hard
Best for: Countertops, butcher blocks, tables

Ash

Light cream to pale brown with a subtle, straight grain. Strong and flexible with excellent shock resistance. A versatile hardwood with clean, understated character.

Characteristics: Light cream, subtle grain, strong & flexible
Best for: Dining tables, countertops, custom pieces

Reclaimed Wood

Wood with history and character. Sourced from fallen trees, old barns, and reclaimed materials. Each piece tells a story and brings unique character to your space.

Characteristics: Unique character, varied species, sustainable
Best for: Mantels, accent pieces, tables with character

Live Edge

Natural edge slabs that preserve the tree's original shape. Available in various species. Creates stunning, one-of-a-kind pieces that celebrate the wood's natural beauty.

Characteristics: Natural edges, unique shapes, dramatic
Best for: Dining tables, mantels, statement pieces
Our Process

How Does It Work

1. Description & Inspiration

Send us a written description of what you’re envisioning, including dimensions and 3–4 inspiration images that capture the look and feel you’re going for.

2. Budget

If you have a budget in mind, let us know. It helps guide material choices, design details, and overall direction.

3. Estimate

Once we review your notes and images, we’ll provide a clear estimate based on your design and budget, outlining the materials and features included.

4. Design Process

We’ll develop several initial sketches. You can select your preferred direction (or request revisions), and we’ll create a detailed digital render for final review.

5. Deposit

A deposit is required before design work begins.

Build Time & Delivery

Timing
