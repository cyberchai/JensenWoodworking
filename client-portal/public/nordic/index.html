About Jensen Woodworking

Custom woodwork crafted in coastal Massachusetts

Handcrafted furniture made with quality and care

Jensen Woodworking brings three decades of craftsmanship to coastal Massachusetts, creating custom countertops, mantels, and one-off furniture commissions.

I focus on high-quality, sustainably sourced materials, often giving new life to fallen trees with unique character. My goal is to build beautiful, functional pieces that balance traditional methods with the needs of a modern home.
